Climbing photography services

Nikita Lambert

I'll tag along on your next trip to the crag and photograph you giving all you've got on the wall. I can go anywhere in the province of Québec and Ontario in Canada.

From up there

One way to capture your visit to the crag is to simply follow you while you climb on the wall. The goal isn't to do a dedicated photoshoot but to capture the action as it is happening. I won't stop or direct you during your climb, just do your thing and have fun. Definitely the way to go to have the most fun!

Craziest shots

To get the best photos, a dedicated photo shoot is needed. It doesn't have to be the whole day, but with some planning and preparation, I will guide you to get the perfect angle, body position and scenery.

Pretty pictures are cool too

At any time, portraits can by taken, no shame in taking pretty photos for Facebook and Instagram!

Where to boss?

I can go pretty far by car, from Gaspésie to the north of Ontario, including Lac-saint-jean and the north of Vermont and Maine. Tell me where to go and I'll join you!
